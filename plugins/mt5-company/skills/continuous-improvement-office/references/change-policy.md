# Change Policy

## Always capture

- what changed
- why it changed
- what problem it is expected to solve
- what was removed or simplified to keep the company lean

## Treat as CEO approval required

- department add/remove
- shared skill add/remove
- shared MCP add/remove
- routing, escalation, or approval-chain changes

## Review outputs

- snapshot diff
- review note
- approval entry when policy-level change is accepted

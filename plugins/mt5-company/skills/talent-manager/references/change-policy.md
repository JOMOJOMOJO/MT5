# Change Policy

## Add a role when:

- A recurring decision is weak without a dedicated viewpoint
- The role has a narrow, stable responsibility
- The role changes outcomes, not just wording

## Remove or merge a role when:

- It duplicates another role
- It only restates what QA or strategy already says
- It has no clear routing trigger

## Always require CEO approval for:

- Shared skill add/remove
- Department add/remove
- Routing or approval chain changes
